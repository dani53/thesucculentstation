Community

Our team is commited to building positive, long lasting relationships within our community to enhance the quality of life and our environment!

Find us at community events such as farmers markets, festivals and art shows where we engage and demonstrate fun activities

Cultivate

Whether you are a new bloomer or Master Gardener, succulents allow us to cultivate new relationships between nature and humans! Attend our workshops to continue learning about succulent care and how to create new projects. Or simply call us! We want to prepare and foster your ever-growing relationship with plants.

Creativity

Creativity with succulents can be contagious! Once introduced to the diverse world of succulents, it is exciting to dabble in the different techniques of propogating and growing succulents. In addition, what seems like an endless list of succulent diy projects on Pinterest, becomes very appealing!
